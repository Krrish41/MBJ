About MBJ Global

The global trading arm of a proven Indian legacy

MBJ Global Pvt. Ltd. is the international trading arm of Shantiratan Empire, a prominent Indian agro-industry group established in 2007. We specialize in both domestic and international trading of premium wheat, flour products, pulses, rice and other agro commodities.

With strong backward integration through multiple large-scale flour mills and advanced processing units, we ensure consistent superior quality, competitive pricing and reliable large-volume supply to buyers across India and worldwide.

Our Vision

To become a preferred global trading partner that bridges the pure essence of Indian agriculture with international markets — while promoting sustainability, quality and food security.
